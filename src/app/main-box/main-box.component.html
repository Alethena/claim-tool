Recover your lost Alethena shares

This page is here to help you in case you lost access to your Alethena shares. It provides you with a simple interface that allows you to reclaim your shares using MetaMask. Before you do anything, please make sure that you fully understand how the recovery process works.

If you are unsure what steps you need to take to recover your shares, please do not hesitate to contact our team at contact@alethena.com.
